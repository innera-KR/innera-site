# Innera Website Spec

## 1. Project Overview

**Brand Name**: Innera  
**Type**: Korean mindset coaching brand website  
**Primary Goal**: Introduce the brand identity, build trust, and guide visitors to the blog and social channels  
**Secondary Goal**: Prepare a clear path for future coaching inquiries, newsletter capture, and content-driven authority building

Innera is a mindset coaching brand designed for people who want to understand themselves more deeply, recover emotional balance, and build a sustainable inner direction. The website should feel calm, emotionally grounded, warm, minimal, and premium.

## 2. Business Context

Innera is part of a larger brand-building effort centered around:

- Mindset coaching
- Authority building through blog and YouTube
- Social media presence and audience trust
- Long-term conversion into coaching, programs, and community offerings

This website is not just a brochure site. It is a trust-building brand home that connects visitors to the wider Innera ecosystem.

## 3. Target Audience

### Primary Audience

- Korean-speaking adults in their 20s to 40s
- People interested in self-understanding, emotional clarity, growth, healing, and mindset work
- Visitors who feel overwhelmed, directionless, emotionally tired, or disconnected from themselves

### Secondary Audience

- People already consuming self-development or coaching content
- Blog readers and SNS followers looking for a more trustworthy brand base
- Potential future coaching clients and collaborators

## 4. Brand Positioning

Innera should be positioned as:

- Thoughtful rather than loud
- Grounded rather than overly motivational
- Emotionally intelligent rather than generic self-help
- Premium but warm
- Quietly confident

The brand should communicate that Innera helps people return to their inner center and rebuild the mindset from which they live.

## 5. Tone and Visual Direction

### Tone

- Calm
- Reflective
- Warm
- Trustworthy
- Minimal
- Elegant

### Visual Direction

- Soft warm neutrals, sand, cream, muted brown, subtle accent tones
- Spacious layout with strong breathing room
- Refined typography with emotional softness
- Minimal but intentional use of gradients, shadows, and surface texture
- Premium editorial feel rather than startup/product feel

## 6. Core User Goals

Visitors should be able to quickly understand:

- What Innera is
- What the brand believes
- Why they should trust it
- What kind of mindset framework or philosophy it offers
- Where to continue engaging with the brand
- How to contact or inquire

## 7. Site-Wide Requirements

- Language: Korean-first
- Design: Responsive on mobile and desktop
- Navigation: Simple and easy to scan
- CTA strategy: Direct visitors to blog, YouTube, SNS, and contact
- Future-ready structure for adding coaching programs, newsletter, and articles

## 8. Information Architecture

The initial page plan should include:

- Home
- About
- Philosophy / NOVA Steps
- Content Hub
- Contact

## 9. Page Plan

### Home

**Purpose**  
Introduce Innera in one glance, establish emotional resonance, and direct visitors into deeper brand exploration.

**Key Message**  
Innera helps people find inner direction and build a grounded mindset for sustainable growth.

**Recommended Sections**

- Hero section with brand statement
- Short brand introduction
- Core pillars of the brand
- Trust-building section
- Featured channel links
- Clear CTA to About, Philosophy, Content Hub, and Contact

**Primary CTA**

- Explore the brand
- Read the philosophy
- Visit the content channels

### About

**Purpose**  
Tell the brand story and explain why Innera exists.

**Key Message**  
Innera was created to offer a deeper, calmer, and more sustainable approach to mindset growth.

**Recommended Sections**

- Founder/brand story
- Why this brand exists
- What makes Innera different
- Brand values
- Emotional promise to the audience

**Primary CTA**

- Continue to Philosophy / NOVA Steps
- Visit Content Hub

### Philosophy / NOVA Steps

**Purpose**  
Present the brand’s core framework in a clear, ownable, and memorable way.

**Key Message**  
Innera follows a mindset process that helps people move from emotional noise to inner clarity and aligned action.

**Framework Page Title Suggestion**

- Innera Philosophy
- NOVA Steps
- The Innera Method

**Recommended Sections**

- Intro to the philosophy
- Explanation of what NOVA means
- 4-step breakdown
- Why this process matters
- How visitors can apply it through content or coaching

**Suggested NOVA Interpretation**

- N: Notice  
  Recognize inner emotions, patterns, and current state
- O: Organize  
  Sort thoughts, beliefs, and emotional signals into clarity
- V: Visualize  
  Reconnect with meaningful direction and desired inner posture
- A: Act  
  Practice aligned mindset habits and intentional action

**Primary CTA**

- Read related content
- Contact for coaching or collaboration

### Content Hub

**Purpose**  
Act as a central gateway to all authority-building content platforms.

**Key Message**  
Innera shares insight consistently across blog, YouTube, and social channels to support visitors in different formats.

**Recommended Sections**

- Overview of content categories
- Blog section
- YouTube section
- Instagram / SNS section
- Featured themes or series
- Optional future email/newsletter block

**Possible Content Categories**

- Mindset
- Emotional resilience
- Self-understanding
- Identity and growth
- Reflection prompts
- Coaching insights

**Primary CTA**

- Visit blog
- Subscribe on YouTube
- Follow on SNS

### Contact

**Purpose**  
Provide a simple and trustworthy contact path for inquiries, coaching interest, and collaboration.

**Key Message**  
Visitors can reach Innera clearly and comfortably without pressure.

**Recommended Sections**

- Warm intro copy
- Contact form or contact method block
- Email / social contact links
- Coaching inquiry note
- Collaboration / media inquiry note
- Response expectation and trust reassurance

**Primary CTA**

- Send inquiry
- Connect through email or SNS

## 10. Conversion Strategy

The site should convert visitors in soft, trust-based stages:

1. Emotional resonance on Home
2. Deeper credibility through About and Philosophy
3. Ongoing engagement through Content Hub
4. Intent-based action through Contact

This approach matches a coaching brand better than aggressive sales messaging.

## 11. Content Priorities

The website should prioritize:

- Brand clarity
- Emotional trust
- Authority through thoughtful content
- Clear next steps

The website should avoid:

- Overly corporate language
- Generic motivational clichés
- Visually noisy layouts
- Too many competing CTAs

## 12. Functional Notes

- Use reusable page section patterns for consistency
- Keep content management simple for future updates
- Leave room to add blog CMS integration later
- Structure navigation and components so pages can expand over time

## 13. Success Criteria

The website is successful if visitors can:

- Understand the Innera brand within a few seconds
- Feel emotional trust and calm while browsing
- Easily move to blog and social channels
- Understand the NOVA Steps framework
- Know how to contact the brand

## 14. Suggested Next Build Phase

After this spec, the next implementation phase should be:

1. Create the multi-page route structure
2. Build shared header/footer/navigation
3. Expand the current Home page to match the page plan
4. Create About page
5. Create Philosophy / NOVA Steps page
6. Create Content Hub page
7. Create Contact page

## 15. Self-Check

- The spec matches the project goal of brand introduction, trust building, and channel direction.
- The page plan includes all requested pages.
- The tone remains aligned with a Korean mindset coaching brand: minimal, warm, emotionally grounded, and premium.

## 16. Error Note

이번 작업에서는 프로젝트 목표를 기준으로 문서 구조를 먼저 설계하고, 페이지 목적과 CTA가 서로 충돌하지 않도록 정리했다.  
앞으로도 브랜드 사이트 기획 문서를 작성할 때는 다음을 먼저 확인한다:
